Astronaut's Coat Rack

This project involved designing a single piece acrylic bracket to hold a 40 pound weight using as little mass as posssible. The bracket attaches to a provided pegboard in two places, the top circle and the middle semi-circle, and the mass attaches at the bottom hole.

Throughout the process of designing and redesigning this bracket, we reduced the mass of the bracket by 68%. In the end, our bracket was able to support the 40 pound mass using only 8 grams of acrylic. The right and left photos show the initial and final designs, respectively.
