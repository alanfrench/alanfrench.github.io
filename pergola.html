Pergola

Four years ago, my parents' nextdoor neighbors cut down their trees. I was mildly upset about our new lack of shade, and my mom offhandedly suggested I build a pergola to replace the trees. I took her up on it, designing and constructing a 6' x 7' x 12' pergla over the course of a summer.

Features:

  • Pressure treated lumber with stain finish to protect against the elements.
  • Used geometric analysis to determine slat and post dimensions.
  • Carved slat ends from hand-drawn template
  • Still standing after four years
