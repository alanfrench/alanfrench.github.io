Walking Robot

This was the third and final project for 24-370 Engineering Design I. The robot had to be able to traverse three obstacle courses: stairs, hurdles, and rocks.

The construction of this robot involved a lot of customization. We laser cut our own legs, gears, feet and body. We designed and analyzed our own coupler curve and four bar linkages, including coupler curve analysis using MotionGen. We repeatedly tested the robot over the course of a week leading up to the final testing day, making adjustments along the way. In the end, the robot was able to complete all three obstacle courses.
